# OpenMaple Code of Conduct

OpenMaple is an open-source managed-agent control plane. The community should stay useful for people evaluating, operating, and extending the platform.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, technical, and respectful.
- Assume contributors may be operating under different cloud, security, or compliance constraints.
- Critique claims, designs, and code with evidence.
- Keep discussions focused on reproducible behavior, clear product boundaries, and maintainable interfaces.
- Disclose conflicts of interest when recommending providers, benchmarks, or integrations.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private credentials, customer data, internal hostnames, or non-public operational details.
- Inflating adoption, benchmark, provider-readiness, or affiliation claims without repo-visible evidence.
- Repeated off-topic promotion, spam, or attempts to redirect support traffic away from the project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that violate this code. Severe or repeated violations may lead to temporary or permanent loss of participation privileges.

For sensitive reports, use the process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). For normal project support, use [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md).
